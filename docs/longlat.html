Longitude vs. Latitude

Above is the aggregate collection of cities used for this analysis. When plotting the Latitudes of each city against it's Longitude, we get a make-shift map of the World! While not every city could be represented due to data constraints, this map shows that we have a very even distribution of cities, which means we can be confident in the conclusions reached at the end of our analysis.
